GrooveBound

A top-down rhythm action shooter built with Love2D.

About

In GrooveBound, enemies approach to the beat of the music. Maintain your groove, aim true, and unleash precise shots on the rhythm to rack up points and chain combos!

Controls

  • Move: WASD
  • Aim: Mouse
  • Shoot: Left Click or Auto-Fire
  • Toggle Settings: Esc or click the ☰ icon

Features

  • Rhythm-based shooting mechanics
  • Customizable BPM and beat subdivisions
  • Adjustable UI fonts and toggles in settings menu
  • Dynamic on-beat visuals and effects
  • High score tracking with combo multipliers
